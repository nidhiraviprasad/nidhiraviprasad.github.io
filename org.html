Codepioneers

In the summer of 2020, I realized that students would be unable to get the summer experience they planned for due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Many programs shifted to recorded videos for instruction, a method which would bore children. Even worse was the number of students who could not afford to pay for good quality programs.

My friend and I started Codepioneers, a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing free and live online programming classes. We designed a hands-on and interactive curriculum, with an emphasis on individual attention. We provide a fun, nurturing, and inclusive experience in each of our classes.

Since the summer, we have hosted 14 sessions and taught over 100 students all over the country and the world. We have taught three levels of Python—Introductory, Intermediate, and Object Oriented Programming—as well as Intro to Web Development. We hope to continue educating students from all over the world in a fun and engaging manner.
