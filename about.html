Who I Am

Hello, I'm Nidhi Raviprasad! I am currently studying Computer Science at Cal Poly.

My goal is to create software which will improve the quality of people's lives. Whether that be in terms of physiology, equity, or simply comfort, I want to make people's lives easier and more enjoyable.

My interests include machine learning and fullstack web app development! I've created multiple projects with ML in Python using the libraries tensorflow, keras, tflearn, scikit, and others. I have also worked with the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, ReactJS, Node), as well as other databases and frameworks.

My journey with programming is far from over and I am very excited to share what I learn!
